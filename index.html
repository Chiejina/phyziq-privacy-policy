Privacy Policy for Phyziq

Last updated: December 14th, 2024

Introduction

Welcome to Phyziq ("we", "us", or "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a secure user experience. This Privacy Policy outlines our policies regarding data collection and use.

Data Collection

Local Data Storage: All your workout data, progress, and personal settings are stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this data, and it is never transmitted to our servers.

Email Collection (Optional): During onboarding, you may optionally provide your email address. If you choose to provide your email, it will be used solely for:

  • Sending app updates and new feature announcements
  • Important service notifications

Your email is stored securely with our email service provider, MailerLite. You can unsubscribe from these communications at any time using the unsubscribe link in any email.

Third-Party Services

If you provide your email address, it is processed by MailerLite, a third-party email marketing service. MailerLite's privacy policy can be found at https://www.mailerlite.com/legal/privacy-policy.

No other third-party services collect user information from within the app.

Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The optional email collection feature is intended for users who are able to provide consent.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Decline to provide your email address (it is optional)
  • Unsubscribe from email communications at any time
  • Request deletion of your email from our mailing list by contacting us

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We encourage you to review this page periodically for any changes. Changes are effective immediately upon posting on this page.
